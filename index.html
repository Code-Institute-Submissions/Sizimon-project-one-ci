About the Game

Objective

In the rift there are three main objectives, the First is to collect gold.
The Second objective is to push forward deeper into the enemy base by destroying their turrets in a valiant attempt to reach the nexus.
The Third objective is to slay the elemental dragons in order to secure match lasting buffs for your team, slaying the dragons alters the game map with new vegetation and terrain changes.

Strategies

Wild rift consists of 5 roles assigned per team; Baron Laner, Dragon Laner, Mid Laner, Jungler and the Support. Utilizing a proper team composition and and teamwork is crucial in order to achieve victory.
It is common practice to use ping alerts to alert your team of any information you acquire which may be the deciding factor in an engagement or push.
Each champion has its strengths and weaknesses and building items suited to your champions strenghts is the most important in ensuring you can be of use to your team.

Map

The Lanes: are divided into three; the Baron Lane, the Middle Lane and the Dragon Lane, AI minions move along these lanes and engage any enemies they encounter. The minions try to push into the opponent teams nexus and it is the laners job to escort them to the towers in order to destroy them.


The Jungle: between the lanes lies the jungle, the jungle has different types of vegetation and its own minions which the jungler defeats to level up, the jungle also features objectives such as the Rift Herald and Elemental Dragons.

Victory

Victory is attained by destroying the enemy team nexus, either by overwhelming them with super minions by destroying their turrets, or just eliminating all the enemy chanpions and destroying the nexus before they have a chance to respawn.


Work with your team, slay your opponents, push the turrets and oblitirate the opponent nexus.
